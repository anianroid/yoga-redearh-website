Chidanand Bubanale

Chidanand Bubanale has over 15 years of teaching experience in teaching yoga. Introduced to yoga early in his life, he has traveled across India to master it in the many acclaimed yoga schools and ashrams. During his extensive travel to different parts of India, he has also completed Advanced Teacher Training courses, Yoga Vedanta and Sadhana

Well-versed in the traditional styles of yoga and adept in the techniques of correct yoga postures, he’s a living personification of the essense of yoga. Lovingly called Chida, by his students and the yoga fraternity he is known for his calm and composed demeanor. His expertise lies in teaching Hatha Yoga and Astanga Vinyasa. He is a registered Senior Yoga Teacher with Yoga Alliance, U.K.

200 Hour Hatha Yoga

Teacher Training Course

Why a Teacher Training Course?

Transform your life with Yoga

Something magical happens when a group of individuals make the decision to gather with the purpose of uplifting their lives & expanding their consciousness. A transformational, inspiring, and life-changing course set amidst peaceful and pristine beaches on the Konkan Coast, a 200-hour course at Red Earth Gokarna is an experience beyond words.

SUBJECTS COVERED

  • Practicals

    Asanas, Pranayama, Mudras, Bandhas, Back bending, hip opening, Meditation techniques Yoga Nidra, Mantra Chanting

  • Teaching methodology

    Alignment, Adjustment, Sequencing, Teaching Skills, Assistance/correction, Instruction.

  • Yoga Philosophy

    Brief study of yoga scriptures.

  • Anatomy

    The study of physical anatomy, the skeleton, organs and muscles, systems.

  • Karma yoga

    Selfless work with mindfulness.

SECLUDED SHORES & WHOLESOME FOOD

  • Accomodation

    Redearth Yoga School is a peaceful and secluded place to learn yogic practices and adopt a holistic yogic lifestyle. Redearth Yoga provides twin sharing luxurious AC rooms, attached bathroom with running hot and cold water supply. The prices are affordable and nominal compared to other yoga teacher training schools.

  • FOOD

    Redearth Yoga promotes a healthy diet. At our restaurant we offer vegetarian food, freshly cooked with love and specially prepared for yoga practitioners. All the food is completely vegetarian, well balanced, comprising all the essential nutrition. There are plenty of other food and beverages options in the menu which you can order on pay basis. Filtered water is available at the dining area.

    Morning Tea time: Brown bread/Biscuits and tea OR fruit salad OR herbal tea OR coffee OR milk tea. Sometimes traditional Indian breakfast will be served too.

    Brunch: Indian food with rice, chapattis (Indian bread), vegetables, salads, sprouts, beans, yogurt, and lentils.

    Dinner: Indian food with rice, chapattis, vegetables and raw salad, and fruits. You are welcome to order a different meal from the items on the menu, at your own cost.

EVALUATION AND CERTIFICATION

Assessments will be based on written as well as practical evaluation.

Evaluation will be carried out only for those students who have a minimum of 90% attendance.

Student conduct will be a part of the assessment criterion.
